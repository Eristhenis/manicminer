806B: Airborne status indicator
Initialised by the routine at 8684, and used by the routines at 870E, 8ABB, 8BDD and 8D05. Possible values are:
Value Meaning
0 Willy is neither falling nor jumping
1 Willy is jumping
2-11 Willy is falling, and can land safely
12+ Willy is falling, and has fallen too far to land safely (see 8BDD)
255 Willy has collided with a nasty or a guardian (see 8D05)
Input
Output
